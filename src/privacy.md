# Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 02, 2026

## 1. Introduction
Welcome to Bank Churn Tracker. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data.

## 2. Data We Collect
We collect information that you provide directly to us:
- **Account Information:** When you sign in with Google, we receive your name, email address, and profile picture.
- **Tracking Data:** We store only the general, non-sensitive information you choose to enter — such as institution names, bonus amounts, and relevant dates. We do not collect account numbers, passwords, or any other sensitive financial credentials.

## 3. How We Use Your Data
We use your data to:
- Provide and maintain our Service.
- Allow you to track your bank bonuses and requirements.
- Authenticate your access to your data.

## 4. Data Storage
Your data is stored securely in MongoDB, a cloud database service. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

## 5. Your Rights
You have the right to access, update, or delete your data at any time through the application interface.

## 6. Contact Us
If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us.
